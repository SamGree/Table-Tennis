IT’S GREAT FOR FITNESS!

Table tennis is an excellent way to get fit and stay in shape. Not only does it give you a full-body workout, but it also helps improve your hand-eye coordination and reflexes. This means that playing table tennis can help improve your strength, stamina, agility and speed—essential skills for any athlete. One of the best things about table tennis is that it’s fun.
